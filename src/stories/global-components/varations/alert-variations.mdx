    # Alert Variantions

    This document provides examples of different variations of the `Alert` component.

    [**> Succes Alert**](#succes-alert)

    [**> Error Alert**](#error-alert)

    [**> Warning Alert**](#warning-alert)

    [**> Information Alert**](#information-alert)


    ## Success Alert

    The **Success Alert** is used to convey positive feedback or successful completion of an action. It typically indicates that an operation was successful, such as saving data or completing a task. 
    This alert type is styled with a green color to signify success and is often accompanied by a checkmark icon.

    <Alert severity="success" title="Operation Successful" onClose={() => console.log('Alert closed')}>
        The operation was completed successfully.
    </Alert>

    ## Error Alert

    The **Error Alert** is used to notify users about a problem or failure that occurred during an operation. This alert type is styled with a red color to signify an error or critical issue and is often accompanied by an error icon. 
    It should be used to indicate problems that require immediate attention or correction.

    <Alert severity="error" title="Error" onClose={() => console.log('Alert closed')}>
        An error occurred while processing the request.
    </Alert>

    ## Warning Alert

    The **Warning Alert** is used to highlight potential issues or cautionary information. It typically signifies that the user should be aware of something that could lead to a problem or requires careful consideration. 
    This alert type is styled with a yellow or orange color to signify caution and is often accompanied by a warning icon.

    <Alert severity="warning" onClose={() => console.log('Alert closed')}>
        This is an important warning.
    </Alert>

    ## Information Alert

    The **Information Alert** is used to provide additional information or context to the user. 
    It is typically used for messages that do not require immediate action but are important for the user's understanding or awareness. This alert type is styled with a blue color to signify informational content and is often accompanied by an information icon.

    <Alert severity="info" title="Information">
        Here is the additional information you need.
    </Alert>
