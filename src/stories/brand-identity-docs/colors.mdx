    # Core Colors

    Our company uses <span style={{color: telcoTheme.palette.primary.main}}>provenant blue</span> (#014db5) as a primary brand color.

    Overusing the blue could give a cartoonish effect, so we avoid filling large background regions with it. 
    We prefer to use neutral canvases that are built from whites and grays according to the light or dark theme that's active. 
    Sometimes we use a faint, translucent wash of our blue over pure white or pure black backgrounds to add chroma and interest;
